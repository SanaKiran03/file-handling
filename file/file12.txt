Hazrat Aisha Siddiqa (RA) is known as the mother of the believers. She (RA) was born in Makkah in the year 614 CE. Hazrat Aisha (RA) is the daughter of the beloved companion of Hazrat Muhammad (SAW), Hazrat Abu Bakr Siddique (RA). She (RA) was the third and youngest wife of Prophet Muhammad (SAW).
